## Step 2: Drop the selected column

WARNING: Deletion is permanent.

1. Click the table name.
2. Click **Columns**.
3. Click &#8942; on the column > **Delete**.
5. Enter "DELETE" in the confirmation dialog.
6. Click **Delete**.
